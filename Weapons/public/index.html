MISSION BRIEFING

You've been airdropped on a small island in the Meta-Caribbean, alone in a strange place with strange people. It's beautiful here but there is trouble in paradise. The last great World War saw the implementation of meta minerals mined from deep within the earth as ingredients to make high quality, technological advanced weapons and ballistics like the world has never seen before. With features ranging anywhere from elemental and magical infusion to unbreakability and non-fungibility.

These high powered weapons and accessories have fallen into the hands of the wrong HODLers. As a member of our super elite weapons recovery team, It is your mission to restore all 10,000 guns back into THE VAULT.

How many will you find?

ABOUT WEAPONS VAULT


The Vault is a Play-to-Earn game and PFP collection, that aims to build a vibrant community and create value for its holders
Weapon holders will be airdropped one pfp per weapon held in wallet
The Vault contains five guns types; Sniper Rifle, Assault Rifle and 3 uniquely designed Handguns (Vult-X37, Vult Bazuka and Velar)
Weapons were generated from 200+ attributes with traits ranging from various line art drawings, color schemes, textures, and more
Weapons were compiled to ensure fair distribution at launch. NOTE: 30 Weapons were reserved at contract deployment
Each weapon costs 0.088 ETH; are stored as ERC721 tokens on the ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS
