WELCOME TO THE WEAPONS VAULT

Weapons Vault is a NFT (Non-Fungible Token) collection of 10,000 weapons. You've been airdropped on a small island in the Meta-Caribbean, alone in a strange place with strange people. It's beautiful here but there is trouble in paradise. The last great World War saw the implementation of meta minerals mined from deep within the earth as ingredients to make high quality technology advanced weapons and ballistics like the world has never seen before. With features ranging everywhere from elemental and magical infusion to unbreakability and non-fungibility.

These powered weapons and accessories have fallen into the hands of the good, the bad and even the ugly. As a member of our super elite weapons recovery team, It is your mission to restore all 10,000 guns back into THE VAULT.

How many will you find?

ABOUT WEAPONS VAULT


The first epic weaponized NFT collection that is building a strong community and creating value for the holders
The Vault contains five guns types; Sniper Rifle, Assault Rifle and 3 uniquely designed Handguns (Vult-X37, Vult Bazuka and Velar)
Weapons will have different rarity levels and the metadata for each weapon is frozen
All 10,000 weapons has been uniquely generated from 200+ different digital attributes with traits ranging from various line art drawings, color schemes, textures, and much more
The weapons were compiled to ensure fair distribution at launch. NOTE: 30 Weapons are being withheld from the initial sale. These will be used for giveaways, rewards and for the creators' of Weapons Vault
Each weapon costs 0.088 ETH; stored as ERC721 tokens on the ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS
